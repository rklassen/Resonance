# Artifact Contract

Status: active contract  
Scope: Resonance workshop artifact intake and identity

## 1. Purpose

An `Artifact` is an identity-bearing input entity inspected by Resonance.

Artifacts are not processes, outputs, requirements, claims, temporary states, or runtime payloads. They are stable source objects from which probe execution, state projection, traces, and reports may be derived.
